About the space

Welcome to Soul Studio Uptown — a 1,200 sq ft loft with 14 ft ceilings and a skylight that brings beautiful natural light all day long.

Furnished with an ivory curved sofa, black leather sofa, accent chairs, grey sofa, coffee table, decorative lamp, high chairs for photos, and paper backdrops. We provide photography equipment and grip to meet your creative needs, plus a kitchen, restrooms, and dressing room.

Ideal for lookbooks, portraits, model tests, family photoshoots, video, and events. See more on Instagram @soulstudio.us.
